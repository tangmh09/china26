Days 1-3 • Dec 22-24

Guangzhou

The Dim Sum Capital & Historical Core

Dec 22 (Tue)

9:00 PM - Arrival at CAN. High-speed rail to city center and Hotel Check-in.

10:30 PM - Midnight snack: Jook-sing Noodles (竹升面) .

Dec 23 (Wed)

8:30 AM - Morning stroll through Yuexiu Park (越秀公园) .

10:30 AM - Traditional Cantonese Dim Sum. Focus on evaluating the "Big Four".

2:00 PM - Walk the tree-lined streets of Shamian Island (沙面岛) .

7:00 PM - Canton Tower (广州塔) area for sunset views of the Zhujiang New Town skyline.

Dec 24 (Thu)

10:30 AM - Explore the Chen Clan Ancestral Hall (陈家祠) .

1:00 PM - Lunch featuring Chu Hou Beef Brisket (柱侯牛腩) .

6:00 PM - Pearl River Night Cruise (珠江夜游) .

Days 4-5 • Dec 25-26

Changsha

Fiery Xiang Spice & Energetic Streets

Dec 25 (Fri)

9:00 AM - High-Speed Rail transit from Guangzhou South to Changsha South (Approx 2.5 hours).

4:00 PM - Dive into the chaotic neon energy of Wuyi Square (五一广场) .

7:30 PM - First encounter with Xiang Cuisine. Seek out Mao's Braised Pork (毛氏红烧肉) .

Dec 26 (Sat)

9:00 AM - Walk the cobblestones of Taiping Old Street (太平老街) , snacking on Stinky Tofu.

12:00 PM - Lunch: Chopped Chili Fish Head (剁椒鱼头) .

8:00 PM - Explore Super Wenheyou (超级文和友) , a multi-story retro 1980s food multiplex.

Days 6-8 • Dec 27-29 PLAN A

Zhangjiajie

The Avatar Mountains & Winter Frost

Dec 27 (Sun)

9:00 AM - High-Speed Rail from Changsha to Zhangjiajie West (Approx 2.5 hours). Zip up that winter coat!

2:00 PM - Take the world's longest cable car up Tianmen Mountain (天门山) . Walk the glass skywalk clinging to the cliffs.

Dec 28 (Mon)

9:00 AM - Full day in Zhangjiajie National Forest Park (张家界国家森林公园) .

12:00 PM - Ride the Bailong Elevator up to Yuanjiajie to see the floating "Hallelujah Mountains".

6:30 PM - Warm up with a massive pot of Tujia minority style Sanxiaguo (三下锅) (a hearty mixed meat hotpot).

Dec 29 (Tue)

10:00 AM - Grand Canyon Glass Bridge or a serene boat ride on Baofeng Lake.

4:00 PM - Rest up, pack your bags, and prepare for the long-haul bullet train tomorrow back down to the tropics.

Weather Backup PLAN B

Fenghuang

The Moody, 300-Year-Old River Town

Trigger this plan on Dec 24 if Zhangjiajie forecasts heavy fog/ice. Cancel ZJJ hotels and book HSR to Fenghuanggucheng.

Dec 27 (Sun)

9:00 AM - High-Speed Rail from Changsha to Fenghuang Ancient Town (Approx 2 hours).

2:00 PM - Check into a traditional Diaojiaolou (wooden stilt house) right over the Tuojiang River.

6:30 PM - Eat Suantangyu (酸汤鱼) (Miao Minority Sour Fish Soup) as thousands of lanterns light up the town.

Dec 28 (Mon)

9:30 AM - Explore the wet cobblestone alleys, Hong Bridge, and the East Gate Tower.

1:00 PM - Lunch: Smoked La Rou (腊肉) (Hunan smoked pork) stir-fried with bamboo shoots.

3:00 PM - Rent beautiful traditional Miao silver jewelry and clothing for a moody riverside photoshoot.

Dec 29 (Tue)

9:30 AM - Buy local ginger candy and handmade silver as souvenirs.

12:00 PM - Board the High-Speed train heading south to Shenzhen to rejoin the main itinerary.

Days 9-11 • Dec 30 - Jan 1

Shenzhen

The Cyberpunk Megacity & NYE Spectacle

Dec 30 (Wed)

9:00 AM - Board the direct High-Speed train from Zhangjiajie to Shenzhen (Approx 6.5 hours).

6:00 PM - Hotel check-in. Explore the cyberpunk/art aesthetic of OCT-LOFT (华侨城创意文化园) for dinner and craft beer.

Dec 31 (Thu) - NYE

10:00 AM - Ascend the Ping An Finance Center (平安金融中心) .

1:30 PM - Lunch: Famous Shenzhen Coconut Chicken Hotpot (椰子鸡) .

8:00 PM - Head to Talent Park (人才公园) for the epic synchronized skyscraper LED light show and NYE countdown!

Jan 1 (Fri) - New Year's Day

11:00 AM - Slow morning exploring the high-end specialty coffee scene in the Nanshan District.

2:00 PM - Relaxing coastal walk along Shenzhen Bay Park (深圳湾公园) looking out towards Hong Kong.

Days 12-14 • Jan 2-4

Shunde

UNESCO Gastronomy Capital

Jan 2 (Sat)

11:00 AM - Short High-Speed Rail transit from Shenzhen over to Shunde.

6:00 PM - Recover from the NYE parties with legendary Shunde Roast Goose (顺德烧鹅) .

Jan 3 (Sun)

10:30 AM - Relax in Qinghui Garden (清晖园) .

1:30 PM - Lunch: Shunde Yusheng (顺德鱼生) (Raw fish slices).

4:00 PM - Afternoon snack of Double-Skin Milk (双皮奶) .

Jan 4 (Mon)

9:00 AM - Morning boat ride through Fengjian Water Town (逢简水乡) .

12:00 PM - Final Shunde lunch featuring Pan-fried Stuffed Mud Carp.

Days 15-16 • Jan 5-6

Guangzhou Return

Final Farewell & Departure

Jan 5 (Tue)

11:00 AM - Short HSR journey back to Guangzhou.

7:00 PM - Farewell feast. Opportunity to revisit any favorite Cantonese dishes or try a grand dish like Roast Suckling Pig.

Jan 6 (Wed)

7:00 AM - Transit to Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport (广州白云国际机场) .

10:15 AM - Departure flight (CAN).
