Days 1-3 • Dec 22-24

Guangzhou (广州)

The Dim Sum Capital & Historical Core

App
Dec 22 (Tue)

9:00 PM - Arrival at CAN. High-speed rail to city center and Hotel Check-in.

10:30 PM - Midnight snack: Jook-sing Noodles (竹升面) .

Dec 23 (Wed)

8:30 AM - Morning stroll through Yuexiu Park (越秀公园) .

10:30 AM - Traditional Cantonese Dim Sum. Focus on evaluating the "Big Four".

2:00 PM - Walk the tree-lined streets of Shamian Island (沙面岛) .

7:00 PM - Canton Tower (广州塔) skyline views.

Dec 24 (Thu)

10:30 AM - Explore the Chen Clan Ancestral Hall (陈家祠) .

1:00 PM - Lunch: Chu Hou Beef Brisket (柱侯牛腩) .

6:00 PM - Pearl River Night Cruise (珠江夜游) .

Days 4-5 • Dec 25-26

Changsha (长沙)

Fiery Xiang Spice & Energetic Streets

App
Dec 25 (Fri)

9:00 AM - High-Speed Rail transit from Guangzhou South to Changsha South.

4:00 PM - Dive into the chaotic neon energy of Wuyi Square (五一广场) .

7:30 PM - Seek out Mao's Braised Pork (毛氏红烧肉) and spicy stir-fried pork.

Dec 26 (Sat)

9:00 AM - Taiping Old Street (太平老街) , snacking on Stinky Tofu.

12:00 PM - Lunch: Chopped Chili Fish Head (剁椒鱼头) .

8:00 PM - Explore Super Wenheyou (超级文和友) , a multi-story retro 1980s food multiplex.

Days 6-8 • Dec 27-29 PLAN A

Zhangjiajie (张家界)

The Avatar Mountains & Winter Frost

App
Dec 27 (Sun)

9:00 AM - High-Speed Rail from Changsha to Zhangjiajie West. Zip up that winter coat!

2:00 PM - Take the world's longest cable car up Tianmen Mountain (天门山) . Walk the glass skywalk clinging to the cliffs.

Dec 28 (Mon)

9:00 AM - Full day in Zhangjiajie National Forest Park (张家界国家森林公园) .

12:00 PM - Ride the Bailong Elevator up to Yuanjiajie to see the floating "Hallelujah Mountains".

6:30 PM - Warm up with a massive pot of Tujia minority style Sanxiaguo (三下锅) .

Dec 29 (Tue)

10:00 AM - Grand Canyon Glass Bridge (大峡谷玻璃桥) or a serene boat ride on Baofeng Lake (宝峰湖) .

4:00 PM - Prepare for the long-haul bullet train tomorrow back down to the tropics.

Weather Backup PLAN B

Fenghuang (凤凰古城)

The Moody, 300-Year-Old River Town

App

Trigger this plan on Dec 24 if Zhangjiajie forecasts heavy fog. Cancel ZJJ hotels and book HSR to Fenghuanggucheng (凤凰古城) .

Dec 27 (Sun)

9:00 AM - High-Speed Rail from Changsha to Fenghuang Ancient Town.

2:00 PM - Check into a traditional Diaojiaolou (wooden stilt house) right over the Tuojiang River.

6:30 PM - Eat Suantangyu (酸汤鱼) (Miao Minority Sour Fish Soup) as lanterns light up the town.

Dec 28 (Mon)

9:30 AM - Explore the wet cobblestone alleys, Hong Bridge, and the East Gate Tower.

1:00 PM - Lunch: Smoked La Rou (腊肉) (Hunan smoked pork) with bamboo shoots.

3:00 PM - Rent beautiful traditional Miao silver jewelry for a moody riverside photoshoot.

Dec 29 (Tue)

9:30 AM - Buy local ginger candy and handmade silver as souvenirs.

12:00 PM - Board the High-Speed train heading south to Shenzhen to rejoin the main itinerary.

Days 9-11 • Dec 30 - Jan 1

Shenzhen (深圳)

The Cyberpunk Megacity & NYE Spectacle

App
Dec 30 (Wed)

Afternoon - Arrive via HSR. Watch the scenery turn back into tropical palm trees.

6:00 PM - Explore the cyberpunk/art aesthetic of OCT-LOFT (华侨城创意文化园) for dinner and craft beer.

Dec 31 (Thu) - NYE

10:00 AM - Ascend the Ping An Finance Center (平安金融中心) .

1:30 PM - Lunch: Famous Shenzhen Coconut Chicken Hotpot (椰子鸡) .

8:00 PM - Head to Talent Park (人才公园) for the epic LED light show and NYE countdown!

Jan 1 (Fri) - New Year's Day

11:00 AM - Slow morning exploring the high-end specialty coffee scene in Nanshan.

2:00 PM - Relaxing coastal walk along Shenzhen Bay Park (深圳湾公园) .

Days 12-14 • Jan 2-4

Foshan (佛山) & Shunde (顺德)

Kung Fu Heritage & UNESCO Gastronomy

App
Jan 2 (Sat) - Arrival

11:00 AM - Short High-Speed Rail transit from Shenzhen.

6:00 PM - Recover from NYE with legendary Shunde Roast Goose (顺德烧鹅) , featuring crispy skin over lychee-wood fire.

Jan 3 (Sun) - Foshan Culture

10:00 AM - Visit the Foshan Ancestral Temple (佛山祖庙) . Watch the high-pole Lion Dance and explore the Ip Man & Wong Fei-hung exhibits.

2:00 PM - Wander through Lingnan Tiandi (岭南天地) , a restored historical district full of alleys, cafes, and pottery.

6:30 PM - Dinner in Foshan city center before heading back to the hotel.

Jan 4 (Mon) - Shunde Food

9:30 AM - Relax in Qinghui Garden (清晖园) , one of the four great gardens of Guangdong.

12:00 PM - Lunch: Shunde Yusheng (顺德鱼生) (Raw fish slices). Followed by a sweet bowl of Double-Skin Milk (双皮奶) .

3:00 PM - Afternoon boat ride through Fengjian Water Town (逢简水乡) .
